The Shorn Ones Association

About

Cloud based community with a focus on historical engagement, data consensus, and group conclusion analaysis. This community will have a specific focus on the Aztec elite warriors called the Shorn Ones, known in classical Nuahtal as the "Cuachicqueh".

Philosophy

Our end goal as a community is to cultivate a historical research-minded cloud atmosphere for curious minds searching for quality social engagement with a focus on history and how it shapes how one identifies with peers, researchers, and public representatives alike.This requires one's ability to bear an open mind with an aptitude to stand firm with their set of beliefs and still have a malleable desire - to better one's research to continue engagement with the community. - to improve one's understanding - to accept a change in one's self-view.
